About 1Ship

Founded in 2015 in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, AhaMove is an on-demand logistics service based on the concept of a shared economy, providing customers to fill excess capacity for logistics suppliers and reduce costs for consumers

Job Discription:

We are currently seeking an ambitious individual to join this team in the role of Product Management Intern.

  • Develops and improves business processes within the technology and business organizations.
  • Perform documentation, conduct user acceptance tests and trainings.
  • Provide technical support, troubleshooting to users.
  • Study of competitive strategy of opponents to propose effective features.
  • Manage, plan and design feature specifications based on requirements.
  • Gather requirements and coordinate team members to deliver the solutions required by operation team and user.
  • Manage Change Requests.
  • Conduct meetings.

Job Requirements

To be successful in the role, you should meet the following requirements:

  • Engineering background or business background with good understanding about technology.
  • Have good analytical and logical skills.
  • Good troubleshooting ability.
  • Excellent customer service ethics.
  • Detail oriented, self motivated and management skills.
  • Strong communication skills – including written and spoken English.
  • Passion for web, mobile design and technology; designing and building simple and intuitive user experiences.
  • Fast learning capability in extremely flexible and adaptable manner.
  • "Get-it-done” entrepreneurial attitude.

Why You'll Love Working Here

  • Disruptive innovations.

  • An open, friendly and transparent international working environment.
  • People-oriented philosophy of doing business.
